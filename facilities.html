Facilities

Always there for you.
  • filter_1Xerox Shop
    Xerox Shop

    The Xerox Shop of VS Hall of Residence caters to everyday needs of VS-ites be it printing of assignments or admit card, a day before exam. Thus it plays a very important role in catering to our academic needs.

    Timings : 10:00 A.M. - 11:00 P.M.
  • filter_2Hall-Library
    Library

    The Hall Library is equipped with abundant books covering a plethora of topics and subjects. VS-ites can issue course textbooks and read everyday periodicals. The hall library is often seen with students coming over to the discuss the projects and stuff panning over a range of fields.

    Timings : 8:00 P.M. - 11:00 P.M.
  • filter_3Music-Room
    Music-Room

    The Music Room of VS Hall of Residence is one place where the music enthusiasts of the hall come to find peace among the songs they love. The music room is often seen bustling with activity during the GC time when the hall tempo is high and the musicians are practicing hard to win in their respective events.

    No specific Timings
  • filter_4Juice Stall
    Juice Stall

    The Juice Stall is one place which has formed a niche of itself in the heart of every VS-ites. Be it fruit-juices, shakes and flavoured milk ranging from everday Bournvita to lip licking keshar badam, this Juice-stall serves it all. The Juice-stall is often a night-time hunger relief to many VS-ites and becomes a place for a quick gossip between friends.

    Timings : 11:00 A.M. - 9:00 P.M.
  • filter_5General Store
    General Store

    The General Store caters to everyday needs of the VS-ites be it graphs, notebooks or pens. All kinds of general items are available here ranging from snack foods to soft drinks.

    Timings : 11:00 A.M. - 10:00 P.M.
